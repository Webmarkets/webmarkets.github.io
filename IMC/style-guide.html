05

Texture & Pattern

✦ The Sparkle Pattern

Sparkling Clean. Literally.

The signature background texture uses 4-point sparkle stars — taken directly from the IMC logo's sparkle marks. At 11% opacity on the brand blue, they create a subtle "freshly polished" shimmer across every page. Clean, bright, alive.

pattern: 4-point star SVG size: 72px × 72px opacity: 11%

The Swoosh Accent

Inspired by the IMC logo's signature blue sweep. Used as a subtle background arc on hero sections, ad templates, and card hover states. Creates motion and energy.

Blue on White Pattern

Sky blue sparkles on white panels. Used inside service cards and hero image overlays. Low opacity keeps it subtle; the pattern reinforces "clean" without distraction.

12

Imagery Style

Bright & Clean

Clean Spaces: Bright, high-key photos of freshly cleaned environments. Well-lit, crisp, and airy. Avoid dark or dingy "before" photos as hero imagery.

Team & People

Friendly Faces: Real team photos and cartoon illustrations. Smiling people holding equipment. Human-first photography that matches the cartoon character energy.

✦ Before & After

Results: Side-by-side before/after shots are powerful proof. Show floors, windows, upholstery. The "after" should gleam — use a blue sparkle overlay to enhance brightness.
