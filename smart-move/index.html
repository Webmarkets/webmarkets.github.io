Project Objectives

Search Engine Optimization

01 Keyword Targeting & Visibility

Smart Move needs to show up where property owners are actively searching. We'll focus on high-intent keywords like "Boise property management," "Meridian property manager," and "rental management company near me."

We'll prioritize geographic and service-based terms with proven volume. By aligning rankings with real search behavior, we increase qualified traffic, not just impressions, delivering consistent inbound leads.

02 Authority & Organic Growth

We'll build long-term authority through strategic content and backlink development, positioning Smart Move as a trusted leader in Treasure Valley property management. Strong authority means higher rankings and more consistent visibility.

Targeted content and backlinks create a compounding effect where SEO produces more leads month over month. The outcome is a scalable acquisition channel with increasing ROI.

Project Objectives

Advertising Management

01 Targeted Lead Generation

Paid advertising will generate immediate leads from property owners across the Treasure Valley. Campaigns target landlords, real estate investors, and accidental landlords in Boise, Meridian, Eagle, and Garden City.

We eliminate waste by filtering tenant inquiries and out-of-area searches. Messaging aligns with owner pain points: vacancy, maintenance headaches, and tenant screening. The result is stronger lead quality and higher conversion.

02 Performance Optimization

Campaigns are continuously monitored and optimized to reduce cost per lead. We test targeting, messaging, and landing page variations to find top-performing combinations based on real data, not assumptions.

Clear tracking provides full visibility into performance and ROI. Every optimization is driven by measurable outcomes, creating a scalable advertising strategy built for long-term growth.
