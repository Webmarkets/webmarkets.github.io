# Orthopedic Surgery in Boise

At Idaho Sports Medicine Institute, we provide advanced orthopedic surgery in Boise for patients dealing with joint injuries, ligament tears, cartilage damage, and degenerative conditions. Our board-certified orthopedic surgeons specialize in treating shoulder, knee, and hip conditions with both minimally invasive arthroscopic techniques and joint replacement procedures when necessary.

Surgery is never our first approach. We prioritize conservative treatment whenever possible. When operative care is required, our team delivers precise surgical intervention followed by coordinated rehabilitation to support long-term recovery and return to activity.

## Minimally Invasive Arthroscopic Surgery

Arthroscopy allows our orthopedic surgeons to visualize, diagnose, and treat joint problems using small incisions and specialized instruments. This minimally invasive technique reduces tissue disruption and often shortens recovery time.

Common arthroscopic procedures include:

* ACL reconstruction using the patellar tendon, hamstring, quad tendon, or allograft  
* PCL and complex multi-ligament knee reconstruction  
* Labral repair  
* Arthroscopic rotator cuff repair  
* Tendon repair  
* Cartilage restoration  
* Osteotomy procedures

Arthroscopy is commonly used to treat sports injuries and joint instability while preserving surrounding structures whenever possible.

## Joint Replacement Surgery

For patients experiencing advanced arthritis or significant joint damage, joint replacement may be recommended to relieve pain and restore function.

Our orthopedic surgeons perform:

* Partial knee replacement, including MAKOplasty computer-assisted knee surgery  
* Total knee replacement  
* Shoulder replacement  
* Reverse total shoulder replacement  
* Total hip arthroplasty

Joint replacement is carefully considered after conservative options have been explored. Our goal is to help patients regain mobility and return to daily activities with improved comfort and stability.

## On-Site Imaging and Surgical Evaluation

Accurate diagnosis is essential before any surgical decision is made. Idaho Sports Medicine Institute offers on-site digital X-ray technology to support timely evaluation and treatment planning.

If you have previous imaging studies, we encourage you to bring them to your appointment so our surgeons can review them alongside your clinical examination.

## Pre and Post-Operative Care

Successful outcomes extend beyond the operating room. Our team coordinates comprehensive preoperative and postoperative care to support safe recovery and long-term performance.

Preoperative care prepares patients physically and mentally for surgery, while postoperative rehabilitation focuses on restoring strength, range of motion, and joint stability. We work closely with physical therapy providers to ensure continuity of care throughout the recovery process.

A Conservative Approach to Surgical Care

While we specialize in orthopedic surgery in Boise, we believe surgery should only be recommended when appropriate. Many musculoskeletal injuries improve with structured rehabilitation and non-surgical treatment. Our surgeons carefully evaluate each case and prioritize the least invasive solution whenever possible.

## Ready to Discuss Your Orthopedic Condition?

If you are experiencing persistent joint pain, instability, or injury that has not improved with conservative care, schedule an evaluation with Idaho Sports Medicine Institute. Our orthopedic surgeons in Boise are here to help you return to activity safely and confidently.
