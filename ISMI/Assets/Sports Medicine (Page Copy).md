# Sports Medicine in Boise

Idaho Sports Medicine Institute provides comprehensive sports medicine in Boise for athletes and active individuals of all levels. Our sports medicine physicians specialize in diagnosing, treating, and preventing musculoskeletal injuries without surgery whenever possible.

From acute sports injuries to chronic overuse conditions, our team delivers evidence-based care designed to restore function, reduce pain, and safely return you to activity.

## Non-Surgical Sports Injury Treatment

Most sports injuries can be treated without surgery. Our sports medicine physicians evaluate ligament injuries, tendon conditions, joint pain, muscle strains, and overuse injuries using a detailed clinical examination and advanced imaging when needed.

Non-surgical treatment options may include:

* Activity modification and guided rehabilitation  
* Bracing and orthotic support  
* Exercise prescription programs  
* Ultrasound-guided injections  
* Platelet-rich plasma injections  
* Viscosupplementation for joint pain

Our conservative treatment philosophy focuses on healing the injury while maintaining strength, mobility, and performance.

Advanced Diagnostic and Injection Therapies

Accurate diagnosis is essential for effective sports medicine care. We utilize diagnostic musculoskeletal ultrasound to evaluate tendons, ligaments, muscles, and joints in real time.

Conditions commonly evaluated with ultrasound include:

* Rotator cuff and biceps tendon injuries  
* Elbow and wrist ligament injuries  
* Carpal tunnel syndrome  
* Achilles tendon injuries  
* Plantar fasciitis  
* Patellar and quadriceps tendon injuries  
* Trochanteric bursitis  
* Iliopsoas tendon dysfunction

Ultrasound-guided injections allow for the precise placement of medication directly into the affected tissue. This improves accuracy and may enhance treatment outcomes.

## Sports Physicals and Injury Prevention

We provide comprehensive sports physicals for athletes in 9th grade and older. These evaluations assess overall health and readiness for participation in organized athletics.

Beyond injury treatment, our sports medicine team emphasizes injury prevention strategies through:

* Biomechanical assessment  
* Strength and conditioning guidance  
* Customized exercise prescription  
* Return to play evaluation

Our goal is to reduce the risk of re-injury while optimizing long-term athletic performance.

Performance Testing and Return to Play

For competitive athletes and highly active individuals, we offer advanced performance testing to guide training and rehabilitation.

Available testing includes:

* Biodex isokinetic strength testing  
* Running gait analysis  
* VO2 max testing  
* Lactate threshold testing  
* Hybrid VO2 and lactate testing  
* Body composition analysis  
* Sport-specific training program design  
* Alter G anti-gravity treadmill rehabilitation  
* ASTYM soft tissue treatment

These tools allow us to objectively measure strength, endurance, biomechanics, and readiness to return to sport.

## Care for Every Level of Athlete

While we treat professional and collegiate athletes, most of our patients are active individuals from Boise and the surrounding communities. Whether you are recovering from an injury, managing chronic joint pain, or seeking to improve performance, our sports medicine team provides personalized care tailored to your goals.
