Rehabilitation Services

Comprehensive Physical Therapy Services in Boise

Below are some of the primary rehabilitation services we provide. In addition to physical therapy, we collaborate with our sports medicine and orthopedic specialists when further evaluation is needed.

Pre and Post-Operative Care

Planning, education, and guided exercise before and after surgery are essential for optimal recovery. Our personalized preoperative and postoperative rehabilitation programs help minimize complications, improve mobility, and accelerate safe return to activity.

Orthopedic Surgery

Manual Therapy

Manual therapy involves skilled hands-on techniques that target joints, muscles, and soft tissue to reduce pain, restore mobility, and improve overall function. Our therapists use their knowledge of anatomy, biomechanics, and movement patterns to tailor treatment to each patient's specific condition.

Therapeutic Exercise

Therapeutic exercises are designed to address specific injuries or limitations and can be adapted for individuals of all ages and fitness levels. These exercises improve strength, flexibility, stability, and functional movement.

Whether you are recovering from injury or managing a chronic condition, structured exercise is often a core part of the rehabilitation process. We also provide specialized programming for senior physical therapy.

Testing and Performance Assessment

In addition to rehabilitation services, we offer advanced testing to assess body composition, strength, oxygen utilization, joint function, and overall performance. These assessments provide valuable data to guide rehabilitation and long-term conditioning programs.
