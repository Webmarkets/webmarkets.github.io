The Eye Associates Difference

Advanced Technology.
Personal Care.

We invest in the best diagnostic tools available so you get accurate answers faster — without sacrificing the personal attention you deserve.

Digital Retinal Imaging

High-resolution imaging of the retina detects early signs of disease — without dilation in many cases. Your full eye health history, preserved digitally.

OCT Scanning

Optical Coherence Tomography provides cross-sectional imaging of retinal layers with micron-level precision — the gold standard for macular and glaucoma assessment.

Electronic Records & Portal

Access your prescriptions, visit notes, and appointment history anytime through our secure online patient portal. No phone tag required.
