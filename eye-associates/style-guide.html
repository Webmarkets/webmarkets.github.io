Background Texture

The Dot Grid

A 24×24px radial dot grid provides subtle visual rhythm without the coldness of a line grid. The cobalt-tinted dots reinforce brand identity at low opacity, adding texture without distraction — ideal for a clinical yet welcoming context.

Dot size: 1px
Grid size: 24×24px

11

Imagery Style

Clean & Bright

Tone: Well-lit, soft lighting. Avoid heavy drama or darkness — this is caring clinical care, not intimidation.

Brand Tint

Tints: Apply a subtle cobalt mix-blend overlay to photography for visual consistency across all channels.

Gradient Fade

Depth: Bottom-up cobalt gradients ensure text legibility over photography in hero and card contexts.

12

Animations

Availability Pulse

Appointment slot indicators, live availability

Confirmation Bounce

Booking confirmed, form success states

Animation Principle

All motion should feel calm and deliberate — never jarring. Use transition-all 0.2s–0.3s ease for interactive states. Reserve animate-pulse strictly for availability / loading states and animate-bounce for positive confirmations only.
