Brand Guide

The visual language, component library, and creative standards for Meridian Window Tint — Idaho's premier 3M Prestige Dealer and Window Genie Partner.

Our Mission

Meridian Window Tint delivers professional-grade tinting solutions for residential, commercial, and automotive clients across the Treasure Valley. As Idaho's only 3M Prestige Dealer and Window Genie Partner, we combine premium products with expert craftsmanship — protecting your investment and elevating every view.

03

Typography

Display — Barlow Condensed 800

Clear Vision.

Usage: Hero headlines, section titles, major callouts. Always uppercase. Pair with tight letter-spacing (0.04em–0.08em).

Body — Barlow 400 / 500

Professional window tinting for residential, commercial, and automotive clients. Barlow brings warmth and readability to longer copy without sacrificing a modern, technical edge.

Usage: Paragraphs, service descriptions, captions, UI labels.

Mono — Space Mono 400

Section Label 02 · Product Code · Spec Value

Usage: Section numbers, technical specs, navigation tags, hex codes.

07

Cards & Panels

Glass Panel

Semi-transparent with backdrop blur. Primary surface for service cards, feature callouts, and hero panels. The frosted look connects directly to our core product.

Dark Panel

Deeper opacity for forms, specs, and secondary content. Provides visual hierarchy without hard borders or divider lines.

Cyan Tint Panel

Cyan-washed glass for stats, trust badges, and featured callouts. Evokes tint film catching light — the brand's core visual metaphor.

Gradient Panel

Subtle cyan-to-teal gradient. Reserved for premium partner callouts, 3M designations, and high-signal trust signals.
