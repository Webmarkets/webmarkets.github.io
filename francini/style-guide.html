14 — Motion & Transitions

Animation Principles

Motion is restrained and intentional — like the slow reveal of a stone slab emerging from wrapping. All transitions use ease curves (never linear), keeping movement organic and refined. Hover states shift in 300ms; page transitions in 500ms.
