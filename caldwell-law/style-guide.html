Body — Inter

Inter is used for all body copy and UI text. It brings modern clarity to the site — ensuring content is immediately legible and never overwhelming. Set at 1.75–1.85 line-height for legal content where comprehension matters.

Usage: Paragraphs, nav links, form labels, descriptions, FAQs.

04

Corner Radius

Standard Radius

4px / 0.25rem

A 4px radius reads as formal and structured — appropriate for a legal brand. Tighter than consumer apps, it communicates precision and professional discipline.

05

Texture & Pattern

The Signature Dot Grid

Refined. Measured. Authoritative.

The brand's background texture is a subtle dot grid in Counsel Gold — each dot spaced at 32px, at near-invisible opacity. It evokes quality stationery, letterhead, and precision — all hallmarks of a trusted legal practice.

pattern: radial-gradient size: 32px × 32px opacity: 7%
