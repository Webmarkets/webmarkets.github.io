Our digital production workflow is built for trade partners — from account setup to job-site delivery, every step is engineered for speed and clarity.

Open a Trade Account

Register as a Greyloch trade partner — builders, contractors, and developers receive priority scheduling, dedicated account support, and volume pricing from day one.

02

Submit Orders Digitally

Orders enter our system digitally — our software calculates optimal material layouts, generates cut files, and queues production automatically. No back-and-forth, no guesswork, no delays.

03

Automated Production

German Homag robotic saws cut, edge-band, drill, and install hardware — automatically. Our facility produces 300+ cabinets per shift with 7–9 workers on the floor and virtually zero defect variance.

04

Staged Delivery & Install

Cabinets are assembled, boxed, and phased to your construction schedule — not a warehouse dump. Our installation teams work in coordination with your supers so every cabinet lands exactly when it's needed.
