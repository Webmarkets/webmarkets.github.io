How Trade Ordering Works

Engineered for
builders. Built
for volume.

From trade account setup to job-site delivery — our digital production workflow is designed to keep your project moving, on schedule, every time.

01

Open a Trade Account

Register as a Greyloch trade partner and unlock priority production scheduling, volume pricing, and a dedicated account manager from day one.

02

Submit Orders Digitally

Orders enter our system digitally. Software optimizes material layout, generates cut files, and queues production — automatically. No phone tag, no delays.

03

Automated Production

German Homag robotics handle cutting, edge-banding, drilling, and hardware installation — 300 cabinets per shift with consistent, zero-variance quality.

04

Phased Job-Site Delivery

Cabinets are boxed and delivered in phase with your construction schedule — no warehouse dumps, no rework. Every delivery is staged for your super's sign-off.
