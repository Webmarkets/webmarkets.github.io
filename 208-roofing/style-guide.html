208 Roofing delivers premium residential and commercial roofing at honest prices — treating every customer like family, never cutting corners on materials or labor, and standing behind every job for years after the work is done.

03

Typography

Display — Oswald

Premium Roofing
Built to Last

Usage: Hero headlines, section titles, CTAs, and all button text.

Body — Open Sans

Open Sans carries all body copy and content. It pairs cleanly with Oswald's bold structure — warm, highly legible, and professional. Always set at 1.6–1.8 line height for optimal readability on dark backgrounds.

Usage: Paragraphs, card descriptions, testimonials, FAQs.

Mono — Roboto Mono

SECTION LABEL 01 — BOISE, IDAHO

Usage: Section numbers, phone numbers, eyebrow labels, code tags, technical data.

04

Corner Radius

Standard Radius

6px / 0.375rem

A tighter 6px radius feels grounded and structural — appropriate for a trade brand. It avoids the overly soft consumer aesthetic while remaining modern and clean.

05

Texture & Pattern

The Signature Slant

Diagonal Roof Lines

The brand's signature texture is built from repeating –45° diagonal lines — a direct nod to roof shingles, ridge lines, and fascia boards. Applied at ultra-low opacity, it adds depth without competing with content.

angle: -45deg repeat: 24px opacity: rgba(255,255,255,0.025)
Default — Light Lines
Red Tint — Hero Sections

Imagery Style

High Contrast

Dark & Bold: Use dramatic, underexposed photography. High contrast reinforces toughness and craftsmanship.

Red Tint

Brand Overlay: Apply a red wash at 15–20% opacity over rooftop or job site photography for consistency.

Fade Out

Gradient Fade: Use a bottom-up fade into the brand dark background for seamless section transitions.
