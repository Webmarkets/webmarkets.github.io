13 — Animation Tokens

Motion System

Animations are purposeful — the drop bob reinforces the water brand, the copper pulse drives emergency urgency, and the ripple expand communicates the brand's local reach. Nothing feels gratuitous.

Hover Lift
translateY(−4px) on card hover. Used on service and testimonial cards.
Drop Bob
The water drop icon slowly bobs up and down. Used on hero and decorative elements.
Ripple Expand
Outward ripple ring. Used on emergency buttons and map location pins.
Copper Pulse
Box-shadow ring in copper. Reserved for emergency CTAs and 24/7 indicators.
Slide In Left
On-scroll reveal for service cards and step items. 0.6s ease.
FAST
Fade Up
Section headers and stat numbers fade up into view as they enter the viewport.
